Market Opportunity

The gap no one is filling

In markets like the US, UK and Australia, there is no public business registry — no equivalent of Norway's Brreg or SSB. Market knowledge is expensive, fragmented, and takes years to build. That is exactly where BrandScout operates.

The Problem

Market clarity takes years. Competitors move in days.

Building genuine market understanding in a competitive, sophisticated market traditionally takes 1–2 years of onboarding and costly consultant engagements. By the time leaders have the picture, the window has closed.

BrandScout's Answer

From years of learning to hours of clarity.

BrandScout compresses market discovery, strategic analysis, and campaign planning into a single session. What took the founder three years of experimentation and client meetings — delivered in minutes.

Primary Markets

USA · UK · Australia — and Nordic companies going global.

Sophisticated, competitive markets where market data is fragmented and expensive. Also the natural tool for Scandinavian businesses entering international markets where they lack local competitive intelligence.

Why Now

AI makes this possible at SMB price points for the first time.

Enterprise CI tools cost $20K–$100K+ per year. BrandScout delivers the same strategic depth — self-served, in minutes — at a fraction of the cost. The technology window is open. The segment is underserved.

Product

Discovery → Analysis → Execution

BrandScout is built around one coherent workflow. Classical strategic frameworks combined with military doctrine — telling users not just what the market looks like, but exactly how to attack or defend their position.

01 — Discover

The Roster

AI-powered competitor discovery. Up to 36 competitors with full intelligence profiles, Listening Posts, Battlecards, and a global Tango Map.

Freemium
02 — Analyse

The Situation Room

Automated 5-step strategic analysis: PESTEL, Porter's Five Forces, SWOT, Ansoff Matrix, Value Proposition Canvas — in minutes.

Premium
03 — Decide

HORIZON AI — Recommendations

Strategic report grounded in the user's own competitive data. Attack strategies, defense strategies, and a prioritised action plan — doctrine-driven.

Premium
04 — Execute

TACNET — Campaign Planning

Converts strategic recommendations into a 3–6 month milestone-based campaign plan with specific tasks, timelines and measurable deliverables.
